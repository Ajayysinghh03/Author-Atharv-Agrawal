Shab-e-Khamoshi


Shab-e-Khamoshi is more than just a collection of poetry; it is an intimate journey through love, loss, longing, and devotion. Each poem is crafted with deep emotions, fl owing seamlessly in an elegant Urdu shayari format. About Me

Atharv A Poet, Lyricist, and Storyteller

Poetry, for me, is more than just words—it is the language of emotions, the voice of silence, and the bridge between the seen and the unseen. I have always been drawn to the beauty of expression, fi nding solace in verses that echo the deepest human experiences. Through my poetry and lyrics, I seek to translate love, longing, and hope into a form that resonates with every soul.


My Journey & Inspirations

My journey as a poet and lyricist began with a deep fascination for words that could evoke profound emotions. Inspired by the timeless elegance of Urdu shayari and the lyrical beauty of music, I strive to create poetry that is both relatable and thought-provoking. Whether it is the aching whispers of separation, the quiet strength of devotion, or the fl eeting moments of desire, my words attempt to capture emotions in their purest form.


Books

My first book, Dastan-e-Ishq , was a heartfelt exploration of an unfi nished love story, tracing the delicate emotions of love and loss. Now, with Shab-e-Khamoshi , I take my readers on a deeper journey —one that speaks of waiting, devotion, and the unspoken emotions that linger in the quiet of the night. As I continue this path, my work extends beyond poetry into songwriting, where I blend emotions with melodies to create music that speaks to the soul. With upcoming projects in both poetry and music, my journey is one of endless creation, where every word is a step closer to touching the hearts of those who seek meaning in art.

Stay tuned for more updates on my latest songs, collaborations, and lyrical works that bring poetry to life through music
